About Us

We pride ourselves in delivering a stylish, modern, and new renting experience.

Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality, fully-furnished properties for short-term stays that are designed to meet the needs of corporate clients, working professionals, or vacationing families. For corporate clients, we offer customized corporate housing solutions for employees on extended business trips or relocations, as well as temporary housing for nurses, interns and new hires. For vacationers, we offer a range of homes that are perfect for families or groups of friends looking for a comfortable and relaxing vacation. We understand that a comfortable and convenient living environment is essential for those who are away from home, and we strive to provide just that.

Benefits of partnering with us

Less Wear And Tear

Experience less wear and tear on your property as our professional cleaning staff ensure that your property is in top-notch condition on a daily basis. Many landlords are surprised to find their property in poor condition after a two-year tenant moves out, but with our professional cleaning staff, you can be rest assured that your property will remain in excellent condition for our guests throughout their stay.

Guranteed Rental Income

When you lease your property to us, we provide you with a guaranteed rental income throughout the entire lease term. You won’t have to deal with the hassle of tenants failing to pay on time or the uncertainty of a tenant losing their job. With us, you’ll receive a stress-free monthly rent payment without any worries.

No Costly Tenant Turnover

Avoid the costly process of tenant turnover when your current tenant moves out. The process can be expensive and includes expenses such as deep cleaning and repainting, listing fees or realtor commissions, and multiple months of vacancy.

More Benefits

Trust And Safety

We prioritize trust and safety, and to that end, we have implemented several measures to ensure your peace and mind. We install video cameras at all entry points of each unit, which monitor 24/7 for added security. Our noise detector devices inside each unit send alerts to our local on-site support staff if noise levels exceed a certain DB level, enabling us to take appropriate action immediately. In addition, we have installed smart locks on each door that generate a new code for each guest, and all codes are deleted upon checkout for added security.

Quality And Care

We employ professional cleaning staff who are dedicated to upholding the highest cleaning standards after each stay. Our team is thoroughly trained, has attention to detail, and takes immense pride in their work. Whether it’s removing tough stains, sanitizing surfaces, or refreshing the ambiance of each room, our staff has the expertise and dedication to deliver exceptional results.
